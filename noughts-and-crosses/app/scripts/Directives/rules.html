Pick which players you want to play, you can have human, AI and random.

  1. Noughts and Crosses is played by 2 people.
  2. The objective is to get a winning line of three noughts or crosses in a horizontal, diagonal or vertical row
  3. Players can have 3 different types of opponents
    • Another human player
    • A pre-trained AI player
    • A randomised AI who will play their turns completely randomly
